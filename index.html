Trézór Hardware Wallet – Getting Started with Trézór.io/start®

Why Use a Trézór Hardware Wallet?

Unlike mobile apps or web-based wallets, a Trézór Hardware Wallet is a physical device that stores your private keys offline. This means no one can access your coins unless they have your device and know your PIN code. Even if your computer is infected with viruses or malware, your Trézór wallet will keep your crypto safe. The wallet itself is small and easy to use. It has a display screen and buttons that allow you to confirm each transaction manually. This makes it more secure and gives you full control over your money..

How to Set Up with Trézór.io/start®

To get started, you need to set up your device using Trézór.io/start®. This guide walks you through every step, including how to: Connect the device to your computer Install the necessary software Create a new wallet Write down your recovery seed phrase This recovery seed is a set of words that helps you recover your wallet if it’s ever lost or damaged. It’s very important to store it safely and never share it with anyone.

Universal Cryptocurrency Support

Manage your diverse crypto portfolio with unparalleled convenience. Trézór supports over 1,000 cryptocurrencies, from major coins like Bitcoin and Ethereum to a wide range of altcoins and tokens. The intuitive Trézór Suite software provides real-time portfolio management, direct exchange access, and detailed transaction history while maintaining absolute security. Advanced Protection Features

Beyond basic security, Trézór offers cutting-edge protection features. The passphrase protection enables hidden wallets for enhanced privacy, while the tamper-evident packaging and secure boot mechanism safeguard against physical tampering. The device's dual-chip design creates true isolation between sensitive operations and potential attack vectors, ensuring your private keys never leave the secure environment, even during firmware updates or system recoveries.
